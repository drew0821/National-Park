Not just a great valley, but a shrine to human foresight, the strength of granite, the
power of glaciers, the persistence of life, and the tranquility of the High Siera

First protected in 1864, Yosemite National Park is best known for it's waterfalls, but
within its nearly 1,200 square miles, you can find deep valleys, grand meadows, ancient
gaint sequoias, a vast wilderness area, and much more.
